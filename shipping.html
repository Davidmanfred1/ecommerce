Shipping Policies

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Custom orders may take 3-5 business days to process.

Shipping Partners

We work with trusted local and international shipping partners including:

  • DHL Express (International)
  • Ghana Post (Domestic)
  • Zipline (Same-day delivery in Accra)
  • Local courier services

Tracking

All orders include tracking information. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

International Shipping

International customers are responsible for:

  • Customs duties and taxes
  • Import fees (if applicable)
  • Providing accurate shipping addresses

Delivery Issues

If you experience any delivery issues, please contact us immediately at hello@akwaabathreads.com. We'll work with our shipping partners to resolve any problems.
