Refund Information

Refund Timeline

  • Processing: 2-3 business days after we receive your return
  • Bank transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Mobile money: 1-2 business days
  • Credit card: 5-10 business days (depending on your bank)

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If this isn't possible, we'll contact you to arrange an alternative refund method.

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, etc.).

International Returns

International customers can return items, but please note:

  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • We recommend using a tracked shipping service
  • Customs duties/taxes are non-refundable
  • Returns may take longer to process due to international shipping

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
